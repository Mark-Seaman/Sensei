My Life Story

by Mark Seaman

Life Experiences

Intro

The meaning of life is a great mystery that requires some deep thinking to understand. My life feels valuable when four different objectives are met. Consider these four dimensions of well-being.

* Contribute * Relate * Learn * Enjoy *

Contribute

One of the greatest joys is to feel that I have made a significant contribution to benefit someone else. This is far more rewarding to me than simply doing something that only meets my needs.

Relate

All people are social beings. Even us introverts need someone that cares deeply about us and whom which we care deeply about. The quality of our lives can be measured to a large extent by the quality of our closest relationships. Experiencing true and deep connection promote overall well-being in every other area.

Learn

Building knowledge and understanding is one of the greatest joys of my life. Learning new skills and new ways of thinking bring purpose and meaning. Throughout my life I have had a love of learning. Curiosity and creativity are very high values for me. I hope to keep growing in knowledge and wisdom for the rest of my life.

Enjoy

Living in the moment, is experiencing life for what it is. I often am so focused on the future and what is not yet a reality that I fail to celebrate or savor what is already happening. I desire to experience life fully as it unfolds rather than miss it and feel regrets. I will embrace reality with both its sorrow and joys.
